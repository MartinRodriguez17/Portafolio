About me

Martin Sebastián Rodríguez Cortez is a student currently graduated from the "Juan Pio Montufar" school with the Graduate Degree in Social Sciences, throughout his last 3 years of Bachelor's degree he has a recognition of public speaking in social expression, Recognition of Student Participation and Academic Representation .

In addition, Martin has several sports awards, including Second Place in the Quito Taekwondo Tournament, First Place in the Cumanda Vive EL Deporte Tournament, and Vice Champion of the Province of Pichincha Sub12.

Martin is currently pursuing a degree in Software Development at the Instituto Tecnológico Superior Quito. He also runs a business that he started with his parents.

Martin has a projection of personal achievements that begins with graduating from the Technological Institute and continuing with his studies to obtain his Engineering, Later his Master's Degree in a foreign country.
